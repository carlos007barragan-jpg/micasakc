Questions

Straight answers

What is owner financing?

Instead of borrowing from a bank, you make your monthly payments directly to us as the seller. We agree on the price, your down payment, and the monthly payment — then you move into your home. No bank underwriting, no mountain of paperwork.

Can I buy with bad credit — or no credit?

Yes. We qualify buyers on their down payment and reliable income, not a credit score. Self-employed, ITIN, and cash-income buyers are welcome. Many of our homeowners were told "no" by a bank first.

Do I really own the home?

Yes. Our agreements are structured for real ownership with properly recorded documents — this is not rent-to-own, where you can lose everything. You receive a year-end interest statement (1098) like any homeowner.

How much do I need down?

It varies by home — typically 10% or more of the price. A bigger down payment lowers your monthly. Tell us what you have and we'll show you homes where the numbers work.
