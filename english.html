What is owner financing?

Instead of borrowing from a bank, you make monthly payments directly to us as the seller. We agree on price, down payment, and monthly amount — then you move into your home.

Do I really own the home?

Yes — real, recorded ownership with proper documents. This is not rent-to-own. You receive a year-end interest statement like any homeowner.

Can I buy with bad credit or an ITIN?

Yes. We qualify buyers on down payment and reliable income — ITIN, self-employed, and cash-income buyers welcome.
